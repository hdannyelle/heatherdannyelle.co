resume

From policy analyst to team leader, Heather’s employers and community have come to see her as a fierce and reliable worker with whom they trust inherently in her ability to deliver strong and consistent results and add value. Heather’s advocacy for Black and brown lives in Berlin and at her university has led to press coverage and a strong relationship with her university’s administration to develop policy solutions to alleviate pain points. Through nurtured partnerships with multiple firms, a strong emphasis on research and storytelling, and a strong leadership, Heather lead the creation and product design of ‘empower’, a new corporate magazine to showcase the Data&Research sector of the non-profit.
